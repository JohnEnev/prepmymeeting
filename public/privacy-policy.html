Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

In Short: PrepMyMeeting helps you prepare for meetings by reading your calendar events and generating preparation checklists. We only access what we need and never share your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • WhatsApp/Telegram Contact: Your phone number or user ID for messaging
  • Messages: Text messages and voice notes you send to our bot
  • User Profile: Your name and username from WhatsApp/Telegram

1.2 Google Calendar Data

When you connect your Google Calendar, we access:

  • Event Details: Event titles, descriptions, dates, times, and locations
  • Attendees: Names and email addresses of meeting participants
  • Meeting Links: Video conference URLs (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.)
Important: We only request calendar.readonly permission. We can read your calendar but cannot modify, create, or delete any events.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate Prep Checklists: Create customized meeting preparation suggestions
  • Send Notifications: Alert you about upcoming meetings 24 hours in advance
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences (checklist length, tone, etc.)
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Data

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) - encrypted at rest
  • OAuth Tokens: Stored securely with encryption
  • Location: United States data centers

3.2 Security Measures

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure calendar access
  • Automatic token refresh (you stay connected securely)
  • Row-level security policies in database
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your calendar events with anyone
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising
  • ❌ Train AI models on your personal data

We only share data with:

  • OpenAI: For generating prep checklists (event details sent to GPT API)
  • Google: To access your calendar (via official Calendar API)
  • Supabase: Database hosting provider
  • Vercel: Application hosting provider

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Disconnect: Revoke calendar access anytime with /disconnect_calendar
  • Export: Download your checklists and conversation history

6. Data Retention

  • Calendar Events: Stored for 90 days after event date
  • Checklists: Kept indefinitely until you delete your account
  • Conversations: Retained for context (last 30 days actively used)
  • OAuth Tokens: Deleted immediately when you disconnect

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Google Calendar API

We use Google Calendar API to read your events. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7.2 OpenAI API

Event details are sent to OpenAI to generate prep suggestions. OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

7.3 WhatsApp/Telegram

Messages are transmitted via Meta/Telegram platforms. Their privacy policies apply.

8. Children's Privacy

PrepMyMeeting is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you via WhatsApp/Telegram of significant changes. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.
